English title
OpenAI introduces “Your Year with ChatGPT” end-of-year experience

English factual summary
OpenAI’s official ChatGPT release notes list a December 22, 2025 update titled “Your Year with ChatGPT,” describing an optional, personalized end-of-year experience that reflects on a user’s activity with ChatGPT over the past year. The feature is presented within ChatGPT as a summary of prior interactions and usage patterns for that individual account.​

Why it matters
This update shows OpenAI further integrating personalized, retrospective experiences directly into ChatGPT itself, using existing interaction history rather than introducing new core model capabilities. For users, it highlights how conversational data can be organized into more digestible summaries, while for product teams it illustrates one approach to packaging AI usage analytics as an opt‑in feature without changing underlying API contracts or developer workflows.
